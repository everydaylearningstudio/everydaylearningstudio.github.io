Daily Brain Teaser - Privacy Policy

App: Daily Brain Teaser

Developer: Everyday Learning Studio

Contact: everydaylearningstudio@gmail.com

Effective date: 2026-05-20

Overview

Daily Brain Teaser is a local-first brain teaser app. The app works without an account, backend account system, cloud sync, ads, or analytics SDK in the current release. The app does use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash diagnostics so stability issues can be found and fixed (see "Crash Diagnostics" below).

Information Stored On Your Device

Daily Brain Teaser stores app data locally on your device so the app can remember your progress and settings. This local data may include:

  • Daily completion progress
  • Current and best streak information
  • Practice progress and Perfect Practice Run stats
  • Weekly Challenge progress
  • Daily Archive entries for completed daily teasers
  • Selected answers and correctness for archived daily teasers
  • Reminder enabled state and selected reminder time
  • Local stats and milestones

This data is stored on your device using Android local storage such as DataStore. In the current release, this app does not send this local progress data to a developer backend or cloud sync service.

Notifications And Reminder Permission

Daily Brain Teaser offers optional daily reminders. If you choose to enable reminders on Android 13 or newer, the app asks for notification permission. The permission is used only to show local daily reminder notifications.

If you do not grant notification permission, daily reminders are not enabled. You can turn reminders off in Settings.

Accounts And Cloud Sync

The current release does not include account creation, login, or cloud sync. Progress is local to the device and app installation.

Ads, Analytics, And Tracking

The current release does not include ads. The current release does not include a product analytics SDK. The current release does not include advertising or marketing tracking SDKs. The only third-party SDK that processes data is Firebase Crashlytics, described in "Crash Diagnostics" below.

Crash Diagnostics

Daily Brain Teaser uses Firebase Crashlytics (a Google service) to automatically collect crash and error reports so we can find and fix stability problems. When the app crashes or records an error, Crashlytics may collect:

  • a stack trace describing where the error happened
  • device model and operating system version
  • app version
  • a Crashlytics installation identifier associated with the report

Crashlytics does not collect the contents of your puzzle progress, archive, or settings. Crash data is processed by Google as part of the Firebase Crashlytics service. For information about how Google handles this data, see Google's privacy policy.

Data Sharing

The current release does not send app progress, archive, reminder settings, or stats to a developer backend. Crash diagnostics are shared with Google through Firebase Crashlytics as described above. Spoiler-free result sharing uses the Android share sheet only when you choose to share.

Data Retention And Deletion

Local app data remains on the device until you delete the app, clear app storage, or the app provides a future deletion option. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data through Android system settings removes local progress and settings from that device.

Children And Target Audience

Daily Brain Teaser is a general puzzle app. It is not currently positioned as a child-directed app and is not currently prepared as a Google Play Families app.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app changes. The published policy includes the effective date above and the contact information below. When the policy is updated, the effective date will be revised.
