Privacy Policy

Block Puzzle (Android) · Every Day Learning Studio

Effective date: 2026-06-22

Contact: everydaylearningstudio@gmail.com

Block Puzzle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you do in the game stays on your device.

This data never leaves your device. We do not send it to any server, and we have no servers.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect, log, or transmit:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Location
  • Crash reports
  • Anything else

Third-party services

Block Puzzle has no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no cloud sync, no accounts, and no crash reporting. We do not embed any third party that collects data about you for us.

The app does rely on two Google Play services that are part of Android. We don't receive any personal data through either; they are operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Google Play In-App Review — after several games the app may show Google's "rate this app" dialog. If you choose to rate, your rating goes to Google Play, not to us. We never learn whether you rated or what you rated.
  • Downloadable Fonts (Google Play Services) — the game's display font (Russo One) is fetched once by Google Play Services and cached on your device. If you're offline, the app simply uses a built-in font instead.

These services do the networking through Google Play Services, so Block Puzzle itself does not request the INTERNET permission.

If we add advertisements in a future version (Google AdMob is the likely candidate), we will update this policy to disclose what is shared with the ad network, request fresh consent under GDPR / CCPA where applicable, and ship a new app version.

Permissions

Block Puzzle declares one permission: VIBRATE, used for the optional haptic feedback on placements and clears (toggle it off in Settings → Audio). It is a normal, install-time permission and does not access any of your data. The app requests no runtime permissions.

Children

Block Puzzle is suitable for users of all ages. Because the app collects no data, it complies with COPPA, GDPR-K, and equivalent regulations by default. There are no in-app purchases, no in-game advertising, and no account or profile creation. The only sharing feature is an optional "Share score" button that opens your device's standard share sheet with a short text — you choose whether and where to send it; the app sends nothing on its own.

Your rights

Because we don't collect any data, there is nothing for you to request, export, or delete on our side.

To wipe the data stored locally on your device:

  • Uninstall the app, or
  • Open Android Settings → Apps → Block Puzzle → Storage → Clear data, or
  • Reset specific data from inside the app: Settings → Reset high score or Settings → Statistics → Reset statistics.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page and bump the in-app version. Material changes — for example, adding advertising, analytics, or any form of data collection — will require explicit consent inside the app before they take effect.
